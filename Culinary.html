🍽️ Culinary Recipes

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Italian herbs

Recipe

Spread tomato sauce over the dough, add cheese and herbs, then bake until the crust is golden and crispy.

Steak

Ingredients

  • Beef steak
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Recipe

Season the steak, sear it on high heat, then finish with butter and garlic until cooked to your preferred doneness.

Khinkhali

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Ground meat
  • Onion
  • Black pepper
  • Broth

Recipe

Fill rolled dough with seasoned meat mixture, shape into dumplings, and boil until fully cooked.

Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Cooking oil

Recipe

Coat chicken in seasoned batter, then deep-fry until golden brown and crispy.

Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Seasonings

Recipe

Coat bite-sized chicken pieces in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, then fry until crispy and golden.

Soup

Ingredients

  • Herbs
